Who We Are

Selfless hearts for the needy foundation Uganda is a non-profit organization committed to improving the lives of vulnerable children, women, and communities through education, healthcare, and empowerment initiatives.

We believe that every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope — regardless of background or circumstance.

Our belief: One opportunity can change a life forever.

Our Story

Selfless Hearts for the needy Uganda was born from a personal journey of hardship and transformation. After growing up in a family that once had stability but later faced economic collapse, the founder’s future became uncertain after primary education.

Through the kindness of a Catholic priest in Masaka and the compassion of an American sponsor, access to education up to university was made possible. Today, the founder is a Medical Clinician whose life and family were transformed through education.

This foundation exists to give vulnerable children and families the same chance — because education, care, and compassion save lives.

Our Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

A transformed society where every individual lives with dignity, hope, and equal opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities in Uganda through education, healthcare, economic empowerment, and humanitarian support.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, accountability, inclusion, and service to humanity guide everything we do.

Why It Matters

Many of the people we serve are not lazy, broken, or hopeless —they are simply forgotten.

We exist to remind them that they are seen, valued, and worthy of care.

Poverty, limited access to education, and inadequate healthcare continue to affect many communities in Uganda. By addressing these challenges, we help restore dignity, build resilience, and create sustainable change.
