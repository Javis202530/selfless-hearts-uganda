Who We Are

Selfless hearts foundation Uganda is a non-profit organization committed to improving the lives of vulnerable children by, providing food, healthcare, clothes and education with the aim of restoring their hope

We believe that every individual deserves an opportunity to survive and hope — regardless of background or circumstance.

Our belief: One opportunity can change a life forever.

Our Story

Selfless Hearts is based in Uganda, and was born from a personal journey of hardship and transformation. After growing up in a family that once had stability but later faced economic collapse and the death of his father, the founder’s future became uncertain after primary education, at 12yrs.

Through the kindness of a Catholic priest and the compassion of an American sponsor, access to education up to university was made possible. Today, the founder is a Medical Clinician whose life and family were transformed through education.

This foundation exists to give vulnerable children and families the same chance — because education, care, and compassion save lives.

Our Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

A transformed society where every individual can get basic needs, hope, and equal opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities in Uganda through education, healthcare, economic empowerment, and humanitarian support.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, accountability, inclusion, and service to humanity guide everything we do.

Why It Matters

Many of the people we serve are not lazy, broken, or hopeless —they are simply forgotten.

We exist to remind them that they are seen, valued, and worthy of care.

Poverty, limited access to education, and inadequate healthcare continue to affect many communities in Uganda. By addressing these challenges, we help restore dignity, build resilience, and create sustainable change.

How We Measure Impact

We track our impact through direct beneficiary engagement, basic records of support provided, and follow-up with families, schools, and healthcare facilities where possible.

Our focus is on tangible outcomes such as school attendance, access to medical care, and improved wellbeing of beneficiaries.
