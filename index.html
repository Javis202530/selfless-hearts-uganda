Who We Are

Selfless hearts Uganda is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting vulnerable children, families, and communities through education, healthcare, and empowerment.

Every child deserves a chance. Every family deserves hope.

Our Story

This foundation was born from a personal journey. After growing up in a family that once had stability but later fell into hardship, the founder’s future became uncertain after primary education.

Through the kindness of a Catholic priest and his friend, a compassionate American, who supported his education up to university. Today, the founder is a healthcare provider whose life and family were transformed through restoring his hope education.

What We Do

  • Education support for vulnerable children
  • Healthcare support for the sick
  • Support for orphans and the elders
  • food support
  • supporting girls get sanitory pads
  • buying requirements for underprivillaged school going children
